Retail Superstore Data Science Project
Overview:
Welcome to the Retail Superstore Data Science Project repository! This project involves an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a retail superstore's dataset, aiming to uncover insights that can inform business strategies and decision-making.

Key Findings:
Regional Sales Disparities:

Identified states with significantly lower sales, proposing targeted discount campaigns to address the imbalance.
City-Wide Marketing Success:

Established the effectiveness of a uniform marketing campaign with discounts across various cities.
Same-Day Shipping for Sales Boost:

Proposed the introduction of a "same-day" shipping option to meet the needs of customers seeking expedited delivery.
Repository Contents:
Jupyter Notebooks:

Sparks_EDA_RETAIL.ipynb: Jupyter Notebook containing the code for the Exploratory Data Analysis.
Insights.ipynb: Notebook summarizing key findings and proposed solutions.
Datasets:

RawData.csv: The raw dataset used for analysis.
CleanedData.csv: Processed and cleaned dataset.
Solution Strategies:

Detailed explanations and solutions for addressing the identified issues.
